What is

GET OFF THE MATRIX

Creativity and Competitivity



Get Off The Matrix is an e-sport club founded in 2021 as an affiliate club of Game Zone, in 2023 the club decided to work independently and focus on different objectives. As of 2023, Get Off The Matrix hopes to elevate competitive e-sports in Tunisia, aiming to find more hidden talents and showcase them to the public. Founded and currently led by Wiem Ben Yahya , GOT'M is situated in Borj Cedreia, Soliman, and has 17 members and 2 e-sport teams.
